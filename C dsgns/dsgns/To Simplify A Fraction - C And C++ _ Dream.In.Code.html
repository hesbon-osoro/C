Re: To Simplify a Fraction

Posted 18 September 2010 - 05:23 PM

You need to find all of the prime factors for both numerator and denominator, then multiply them out again but without the common factors.
Example:
30/100 = (2*3*5) /(2*2*5*5)
removing a 2 and a 5 from both numerator and denominator leaves us with:
3 /(2*5) = 3/10

Now do this with code!

Prime factors are nice. However, common factors, period, are enough to simplify.

Just keep taking common factors out of both until your run out of possibles in the denominator. And, yes, the modulus operator is your friend.
